• March 26, 1984
    Today was an ordinary day in the Research Department. Winston and I had a great talk about The Eleventh Edition. He doesn't understand the beauty of Newspeak, all he wants to do is speak the same old Oldspeak. Who needs words like splendid when we could just use doubleplusgood! I worked on removing some more words. You can't commit thoughtcrime if we replace the word dad with unmom.
