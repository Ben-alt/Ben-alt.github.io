The Problem

Governments and big companies are collecting all of our information. This is threatening our anonymity as they are collecting everything you do on the internet. Location, browsing habits, search history, etc. This allows the company or government to keep tabs on everyone and do whatever they want with this data (for example, sell it to other companies). This data can also be vulnerable to hackers through interception or data breaches.

In 2017, 1,579 were reported. This is double the amount of data breaches than in 2014. The number of breaches doesn't ammount to the impact of the breaches.
